Keto Pizza

Description

This pizza is perfect for someone who is need of a quick meal but without sacrificing their macros to stay in ketosis. This recipe can be made with an air fryer or standard oven and completed in under 45-minutes. We will be using almond flour, this particular brand contains 4g of carbs per serving size, so it is perfect for your keto macros.

Macros

  • Calories: 434
  • Fat: 38g
  • Carbohydrates: 7g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Protein: 17g

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp cottage cheese(sour cream can be substituted)
  • 2 Tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp pink Himalayan or sea salt
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven or air fryer to 400 degrees and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl. whisk together the eggs, cottage cheese, and butter. Set aside.
  3. In a seperate bowl, whisk together the almond flour, garlic powder, and salt. Add dry micture to wet ingredients and stir to combine. Mixture will be wet. Stir in the cheddar cheese.
  4. Roll dough between two parchment paper sheets to desired size, thickness, and shape. the dough will be very wet. You will need to peel the top parchment sheet off and bake it on the bottom sheet of parchment on a baking sheet. Bake for 12-14 minutes or until the desired level of crispiness. The longer you bake it the crispier it gets!
  5. Remove from the oven and load your pizza however you like wiht all of your favorite sauces, cheese, and toppings. Bake pizza for another 5-8 minutes or until the cheese is melted. Enjoy!
